Executive Summary

The "[airline] [city] flights 2026" keyword pattern is experiencing an unprecedented, crisis-driven breakout across Google Trends. Virtually every airline+Gulf city combination spiked 5,000%+ in early March 2026, driven by the US-Iran conflict and Gulf airspace closures. The India-Gulf corridor dominates — Air India, IndiGo, Emirates, and Qatar Airways route keywords show the strongest demand. This is a massive content opportunity: travelers are actively searching for specific airline+route status information, and Wego can capture this intent with targeted route-specific articles that include flight tracking links. The pattern extends beyond crisis: new route launches (Riyadh Air, Flyadeal) and SE Asian rerouting are generating sustained search interest that will outlast the crisis.

How to Read This Report

All keywords follow the pattern "[airline name] [city name] flights 2026". Growth is measured from Google Trends data over the past 90 days (Dec 26, 2025 – Mar 26, 2026). Because most of these keywords had zero search volume before the crisis, nearly all qualify as "Breakout" (5,000%+). We've ranked them by relative peak search interest within comparison sets and cross-referenced against absolute volume indicators (regional interest scores). Keywords compared within the same Trends query share a relative scale; cross-set comparisons are approximate.
